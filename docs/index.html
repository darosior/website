About

I work on Bitcoin at Chaincode Labs. My work has recently been focused around enhancing the robustness of the protocol and the security of its implementations. I previously co-founded Wizardsardine, of which I am still a shareholder. There i researched Bitcoin vaults and implemented Revault, a Bitcoin vault for multi-party custody. I also maintained Liana, a Bitcoin wallet aimed at reducing risks of loss. Before that i co-founded Bitcoin-Lyon, a local Bitcoin broker in Lyon, France. I also used to contribute to Lightning.

I’m interested in information security, economics and politics.
